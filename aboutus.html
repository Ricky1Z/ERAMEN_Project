ABOUT US
ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All ERamen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura. Our vision is to be the leading ramen shop with the best quality and great customer experience and our mission is to provide high-quality, innovative ramen and satisfy customers with the best service.
